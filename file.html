Options

Sort the Numbers

Instructions:

Select the smallest number from the "Options" section by clicking and holding the mouse button. Drag the selected number and drop it into the "Sort the Numbers" section on the right side. Repeat the process for the remaining numbers, selecting the next smallest number each time and placing next to the previously sorted numbers in the "Sort the Numbers" section. Keep dragging and dropping numbers until all numbers from the "Options" section are in the "Sort the Numbers" section.
